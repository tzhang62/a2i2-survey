üìù Survey Content

Section 1: Background Information

Collects basic demographic information including email (optional), nickname, age, gender, education level, occupation, and political ideology (7-point scale).

Section 2: Personality Traits

10 questions measuring the Big Five personality dimensions: Extraversion, Agreeableness, Conscientiousness, Neuroticism, and Openness. Uses a 5-point Likert scale.

Section 3: Moral Foundations

12 questions based on Moral Foundations Theory, measuring Care, Fairness, Loyalty, Authority, Sanctity, and Liberty. Uses a 6-point Likert scale.

Section 4: Special Needs

Assesses physical/medical conditions, responsibility for others, and need for special assistance during emergency evacuation.
